@wardens Based on couple of questions I have received, I'd like to clarify one assumption we are making for the contracts (which is enforced at the axelar proof of stake network): Operators correspond to validators on the Axelar network. However, the operator address for a given epoch is derived from the validator key along with a nonce that is unique for each operator epoch and chain. i.e Whenever operatorship is being transferred, an honest validator will always generate a new operator address (and not reuse their old one) due to a nonce. (edited)
